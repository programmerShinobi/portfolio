# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/),
version 2.1.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Examples of positive behavior: empathy, respect for differing opinions,
constructive feedback, and accountability.

Unacceptable behavior: harassment, insults, trolling, or publishing others'
private information.

## Enforcement

Report concerns to the maintainer at the email listed in the portfolio. All
reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.

For the full text, see <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.
